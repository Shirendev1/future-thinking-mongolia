# Future Thinking - Mongolia as a Global Model
## Vision
Future Thinking exists to demonstrate that Mongolia can become a global model-
where human values, nature, and technology ceoxist in harmony.
## Core Idea
**From Energy Consumer > Digital Infrastructure Competitor**
## Key pillars
-Renewable energy
-Energy storage (key pillar)
-Green data centers
-Cold-climate advantage
## The problem we address
- Mongolia is positioned mainly as a raw- material exporter
- Energy is produced , but its value is not multiplied
- Data is hosted outside the country
- Development is often associated with environmental degratation

Work in progress
